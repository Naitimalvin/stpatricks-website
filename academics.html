Academics

We offer a diverse curriculum that balances core academics, sciences, practical subjects, and student development through extracurriculars and counseling.

  • Subjects: Shona, Mathematics, English, History, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Heritage Studies, Principles of Accounting, Religious Studies, Geography, Computer Science
  • Practicals: Fashion and Fabrics, Woodworking Technology, Agriculture
  • Clubs: LEO Club, Interact Club, Choir
  • Sports: Basketball, Football (Soccer), Handball, Netball, Volleyball, Athletics (High Jump, Long Jump, Long Distance Running, 100m Sprint, Tag Running)
  • Support: Weekly guidance and counselling sessions included in the timetable
